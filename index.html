We started Hyperkey™ to solve a problem that seemed invisible until it was obvious. Yet people spend more time on their mobile devices in chat than anywhere else. Why not make the services you use available in the place you spend the most time?

Share your emotions!

Now you can start or end a message with a visual bang. There are times when a image is worth more than 1,000 words. With Hyperkey the exploding new universe of visual communications is at your fingertips.

Be more productive!

Important files of your life and work are in Facebook, Instagram, Google Drive, and elsewhere. With Hyperkey you can browse your cloud accounts and select images and documents to share all while you chat.

Get results fast!

Find the information you depend on without leaving chat. When you need to search for an answer you can do it on the Hyperkey keyboard, and share the results with the person you are talking to.

Play with friends in chat!

Simply start a game with your chat partner or send an invite to a friend. More fun than solitaire :). You can play Hyperkey games with your fiends, win or lose, as if you were in the same room, sitting across a table.

Pay in an instant!

Hyperkey saves you time when you have to settle a restaurant bill with your friends, or make a quick payment. You can send or request payments with your Venmo account.
